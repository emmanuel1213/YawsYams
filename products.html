Our Fresh Ghanaian Produce

Premium export-grade staples from trusted farmer networks, handled with strict quality control.

Yams

PREMIUM PUNA YAMS and other varieties (Nyame Nti, Olodo, Water yam). Carefully graded for export.

Groundnuts

ORGANIC GROUNDNUTS, cleaned and shelled, supplied in moisture-safe sacks for long-haul logistics.

Beans

QUALITY BEANS (cowpea) selected for uniform size and color; debris removed to buyer specifications.

FRESH AGUSHI (melon seed) winnowed and dried to preserve flavor; export-ready packaging available.

Packaging & Specs

Standard export packaging: 20kg–25kg cartons or buyer-specified bulk bags. Phytosanitary and certificates of origin provided.
